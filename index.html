--- title: "Intro to R" author: "Helen Phillips" date: "May 30, 2017" output: html_document --- # Introduction to R ## Getting Started In order to go through these notes you will need to have downloaded both [R](https://cran.r-project.org/) and [RStudio](https://www.rstudio.com/products/rstudio/download). If you need extra guidance please read [this help page](http://www.datacarpentry.org/R-ecology-lesson/index.html#setup_instructions). ## Why learn R? * Reproducibility - for your future self, but also for anyone else * Easy to see what you've done - can spot mistakes * A deeper understanding of what you are doing * Can work with lots of data easily * Production of quality figures * It's free!
